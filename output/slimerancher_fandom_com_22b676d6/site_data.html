Slime Rancher 2 is an indie open-world first-person life simulation video game developed and published by Monomi Park. The game is a direct sequel to Slime Rancher and features Beatrix LeBeau exploring the new Rainbow Island after a mysterious boat brought the protagonist over the slime sea. The game was released for early access on September 22nd, 2022 on Steam, and Epic Game Store while Xbox Series X/S and PlayStation 5 were given In-Game Previews. Slime Rancher 2 was released into early access for PlayStation 5 on June 11th, 2024.

The game ended early access and dropped full release on September 23rd, 2025. [1]

Description[]

Continue the adventures of Beatrix LeBeau as she journeys to Rainbow Island, a mysterious land brimming with ancient technology, unknown natural resources, and an avalanche of wiggling, jiggling, new slimes to discover, like the bouncy Cotton Slime or the aquatic Angler Slime. As Beatrix attempts to unravel the islands secrets and uncover its true purpose, shell build, ranch, and farm within a beautiful conservatory, whose sparkling glass walls give her full view of the prismatic paradise she now calls home.
